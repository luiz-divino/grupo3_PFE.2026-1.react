# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Professionals, executives, board members, companies, students, and researchers interested in corporate governance in Brazil. They visit the site to understand ACBrasil, read institutional content, watch webinars, contact the team, or start an association request.

## Product Purpose

The site presents ACBrasil as a credible institutional authority, communicates its mission, and guides visitors toward learning, contacting the association, or becoming associated. Success means visitors can quickly understand the organization, trust the content, and use public forms without accessibility barriers.

## Brand Personality

Credible, clear, institutional. The interface should feel confident and professional while preserving the current blue and yellow identity.

## Anti-references

Do not redesign the site into a generic SaaS template, over-polished glassmorphism, or a visually detached palette. Do not remove the recognizable ACBrasil blue/yellow brand language when fixing accessibility.

## Design Principles

- Preserve recognition: keep the established ACBrasil color identity and institutional tone.
- Lead with clarity: text, CTAs, and forms must be readable before they are decorative.
- Earn trust through restraint: adjust contrast and hierarchy without unnecessary visual churn.
- Keep public paths inclusive: core institutional pages, articles, webinars, contact, and association flows should meet WCAG AA contrast.
- Let imagery support content: any text over photos needs a stable overlay, not hope that the photo happens to be dark enough.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for public routes. Normal text must meet at least 4.5:1 contrast; large text and essential graphical UI elements must meet at least 3:1. Placeholder text should also meet 4.5:1. Color choices should remain legible for users with low vision or color-vision differences, especially in hero sections, CTAs, badges, and forms.
